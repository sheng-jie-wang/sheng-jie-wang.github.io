Biography

I'm a final-year Ph.D student in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Washington, advised by Jeff Bilmes. I'm interested in machine learning in general including fields such as deep learning, submodular optimization, computer vision, and NLP. I received my bachelor degree from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
